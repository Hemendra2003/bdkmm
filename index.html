THE ENGINE
The operating system

You don't have a
discipline problem.

You have a physics problem. Momentum is real — it compounds, it decays, and it responds to every single choice you make each day.

01
Velocity, not completion
Most apps track tasks done. Momentum tracks speed. Your velocity is a single number accumulating across every day. No reset. Just the rocket you've been building.
THRUST
Gym. Deep work. Good sleep. Heavy meals for bulk. Each adds real acceleration.
DRAG
Doomscrolling. Skipped routines. Poor mood. These subtract velocity you already earned.
